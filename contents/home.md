I am a PhD student in Computing Science at the University of Glasgow, supervised by Dr. Mary Ellen Foster and Dr. Peggy Gregory. My research interests lie in deploying robots within real-world, public space settings. I am interested in how social robots can be integrated into existing workflows within organisations to tackle concrete business problems, and not just to simply provide gratuitious interactions. I seek to understand how we can better design social robots for public spaces through human-centric design processes and wide stakeholder engagement.
